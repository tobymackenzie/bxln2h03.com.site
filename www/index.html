Be Excellent to Each Other

…and party on, dudes.

- Abraham Lincoln, Ted "Theodore" Logan,
and Bill S Preston Esquire, Bill & Ted's Excellent Adventure

Three great wise men gave us this most excellent proclamation to help guide us in a most triumphant life. Do well unto others while enjoying life, they say. Following in the footsteps of The Golden Rule, these words, by themselves, do not tell give us wisdom, but serve as a shortcut telling us what we should do. When in doubt, think of these words, and abide them.
